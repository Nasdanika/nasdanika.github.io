Alignment

An association class between aims and engineered capabilities. Aims can be aligned to higher level aims and engineered capabilities can be aligned to aims.

Examples:

  • A team objective can be aligned to a company’s objective key result. The company objective can in turn can be aligned to a company or persona goal.
  • Feature can be aligned to an objective or a goal.
